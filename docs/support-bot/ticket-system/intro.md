## How does the ticket system work?

The ticket system of the SCNX Support-Bot works basically like other ticket systems you may have come across before. However, we’ve developed new innovative features
that will save you and your team members a lot of work.

To give you a better overview, we’ve split the individual features in this article into different sections.
Of course, you’ll also find every feature explained in detail on the respective documentation pages.
