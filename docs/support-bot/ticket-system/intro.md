## How does the ticket system work?

The SCNX Support-Bot ticket system works similarly to other ticket systems you may have used before, but we’ve developed new innovative features that will save you and your team members a lot of work.

To give you a better overview, we’ve split the individual features in this article into multiple sections. Of course, you’ll also find every feature explained in detail on the respective documentation pages.
