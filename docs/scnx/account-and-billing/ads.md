# Ads on SCNX

Advertise your server or product on SCNX and reach a young tech-affine audience consisting of Discord power users. SCNX Ads help finance free bot hosting for the community.

## Ad Types

### Server Checkin Ads

Checkin ads are shown to users when they extend their free server plan (weekly checkin). These ads reach users who are actively managing their Discord servers.

- **Format:** 15-120 second video or up to 4 images with text overlay
- **Targeting:** Shown during the weekly server checkin process
- **Pricing:** Charged per click and per view
- **Link to:** A website URL or an SCNX-enabled Discord server

### Dashboard Ads

Dashboard ads are displayed as small banners in the bottom-right corner of the SCNX dashboard while users browse their servers.

- **Format:** Text + image
- **Targeting:** Shown to dashboard users approximately every 30 seconds
- **Pricing:** Charged per 1,000 views and per click
- **Link to:** A website URL or a Discord server

## Creating an Ad

1. Go to [scnx.app/user/ads](https://scnx.app/user/ads)
2. Click **Create Ad** and select the ad type
3. Fill in the ad content:
   - **Text:** The message shown alongside your ad
   - **Media:** Upload a video (for checkin ads) or images
   - **Target:** Choose whether to link to a website or a Discord server
   - **Locale:** Select which language audience to target
4. Set your payment preferences (see [Payment & Budgeting](#payment--budgeting))
5. Submit for review

## Ad Review

All ads are reviewed by the SCNX team before going live. This usually takes up to 12 hours. Your ad status will show as **Review Pending** during this time.

Ads may be denied if they violate content guidelines (hate speech, misleading claims, political content, etc.).

## Ad States

| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Active** | Running and being shown to users |
| **Paused** | Manually paused by you, no charges |
| **Out of Funds** | Monthly spending limit reached or balance exhausted |
| **Review Pending** | Awaiting staff review (up to 12 hours) |
| **Denied** | Rejected for content guideline violation |
| **Invalid** | Content no longer valid (e.g. Discord server removed, bot permissions changed) |
| **Archived** | Permanently removed |

## Managing Your Ads

From the [ads dashboard](https://scnx.app/user/ads), you can:

- **Pause/Resume** ads at any time
- **Edit content** (text, images, target URL, locale)
- **Update payment settings** (spending limits, payment method)
- **View performance** (clicks and views, both monthly and lifetime)
- **Archive** ads you no longer need

## Payment & Budgeting

### Ad Balance

Ads are funded from your **ad balance** - a prepaid credit on your SCNX account. You can top up your balance from the ads dashboard.

### Payment Methods

- **Pre-Paid Without Limit:** Your ads run until your balance runs out
- **Pre-Paid With Limit:** Set a monthly spending cap - ads pause when the limit is reached

### Pricing

| Ad Type | Cost per Click | Cost per View |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| Checkin Video | 0.02 EUR | 0.01 EUR |
| Dashboard Ad | 0.01 EUR | 0.003 EUR (per 1,000 views) |

## Video Specifications

For checkin video ads:

- **Max file size:** 40 MB
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9
- **Max resolution:** 1280x720
- **Duration:** 15-120 seconds
