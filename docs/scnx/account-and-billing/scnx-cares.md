# SCNX Cares

At ScootKit, we believe that when something goes wrong, it's our responsibility to make it right. **SCNX Cares** is our customer accommodation program designed to acknowledge service disruptions, resolve issues transparently, and ensure you feel valued as a member of our community.

## What is SCNX Cares?

When you experience a service issue on SCNX - whether it's unexpected downtime, a software bug, a billing discrepancy, or a delayed support response - our team may issue you a **Care Voucher**. This voucher allows you to select a complimentary accommodation as our way of apologizing for the inconvenience.

## How It Works

1. **We identify the issue**: Our support team reviews your case and determines the appropriate resolution.
2. **You receive a Care Voucher**: You'll get a personalized email from `care@scnx.xyz` explaining what happened, what we've done to fix it, and a link to redeem your voucher.
3. **You'll also see a banner** on your SCNX dashboard letting you know a voucher is waiting for you.
4. **Choose your accommodation**: Visit the redemption page and select the option that works best for you.
5. **Instantly applied**: Your chosen accommodation is applied to your account immediately.

## What Accommodations Are Available?


:::info
The specific options available to you depend on your current plan and the nature of the issue. You choose **one** accommodation per voucher.
:::


Depending on the severity of the issue, you may be offered one of the following options:

| Accommodation | Description |
|---|---|
| **Plan Extension** | Additional days added to your current plan at no charge |
| **Account Balance Credit** | A credit applied to your SCNX balance for future purchases |
| **Free Plan Trial** | Try a higher-tier plan (Unlimited or Professional) for free |
| **AI Coins** | Free AI coins for your server's AI features |
| **Membership** | A complimentary Level 2 or Level 3 membership |

## How Do I Redeem My Voucher?

1. Click the link in your Care email, or click the banner on your SCNX dashboard
2. Review the incident details and our apology
3. Select your preferred accommodation from the available options
4. If no server was pre-selected, choose which server should receive the accommodation
5. Confirm your selection

Your accommodation will be applied instantly and you'll receive a confirmation email.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why did I receive a Care Voucher?

You received a voucher because our team identified that you were affected by a service issue. The email accompanying your voucher explains the specific incident.

### How long is my voucher valid?

Care Vouchers are valid for **14 days** from the date they are issued. After this period, they expire automatically.

### Can I choose more than one accommodation?

No, each voucher allows you to select **one** accommodation. If multiple servers were affected, you may receive multiple vouchers.

### I have a subscription - how does a plan extension work?

For subscription users, a plan extension delays your next billing date. You won't be charged until the extended date, and your subscription remains fully active during this period.

### Can I transfer my voucher to someone else?

No, Care Vouchers are tied to your account and cannot be transferred.

### I didn't receive an email - what should I do?

Check your spam folder for emails from `care@scnx.xyz`. You can also check your SCNX dashboard for a banner notification. If you believe you should have received a voucher, please contact our support team at [scnx.app/help](https://scnx.app/help).

### What happens after I redeem my voucher?

You'll receive a confirmation email, and the accommodation is applied immediately. We may also ask you to rate your experience with a quick 1-5 star rating to help us improve.

### I have more questions about a specific incident.

Please contact our support team at [scnx.app/help](https://scnx.app/help) and reference the ticket ID mentioned in your Care email.
