# The SCNX Custom Discord Bot

The fully customizable Discord general-purpose Bot - hosted for free on SCNX. Dive deep into thousands of configuration
options and create a unique experience on your server.

## Overview {#overview}

The SCNX Custom-Bot is an all-in-one Discord bot with over 65 individually toggleable modules — from moderation and
leveling to AI chat, games, social media notifications, and much more. It's set up in minutes, hosted 24/7 for free, and
managed entirely through the [SCNX Dashboard](https://scnx.app).

## What you can do {#capabilities}

* **[Moderation](/docs/custom-bot/modules/moderation) & Safety** — A complete moderation toolkit with manual
  commands, auto-moderation (spam, scam links, word blacklist, anti-raid), warning escalation,
  [logging](/docs/custom-bot/modules/administration/logging), and more.
* **[Leveling & XP](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/levels)** — Reward active members with XP from messages and
  voice chat. Configure role rewards, XP multipliers, leaderboards, and custom leveling curves.
* **[Economy System](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/economy-system)** — A full virtual economy with work, crime,
  robberies, banking, daily bonuses, message drops, and a server shop.
* **[Tickets](/docs/custom-bot/modules/support/tickets) & Support** — Multi-category ticket system with transcripts,
  [forum support](/docs/custom-bot/modules/support/forum-support), and multi-question
  [applications](/docs/custom-bot/modules/support/applications).
* **Welcome & Member Management** — Customizable [welcome/leave messages](/docs/custom-bot/modules/administration/welcomer),
  auto-roles, [birthday](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/birthday) tracking,
  [reaction roles](/docs/custom-bot/modules/bot/reaction-roles), button roles,
  [AFK system](/docs/custom-bot/modules/tools/afk-system), and more.
* **Social Media Notifications** — Get notified when creators post on
  [Twitch](/docs/custom-bot/modules/integrations/twitch-notifications),
  [YouTube](/docs/custom-bot/modules/integrations/youtube-notifications),
  [Reddit](/docs/custom-bot/modules/integrations/reddit-notifications),
  [RSS feeds](/docs/custom-bot/modules/integrations/rss-notifications),
  [Threads](/docs/custom-bot/modules/integrations/threads-notifications), and
  [TikTok](/docs/custom-bot/modules/integrations/tiktok-notifications).
* **[AI Features](/docs/custom-bot/modules/ai)** — AI-powered chat channel with custom personalities, AI image
  generation, and Akinator.
* **Games & Fun** — [Connect Four](/docs/custom-bot/modules/fun/connect-four),
  [UNO](/docs/custom-bot/modules/fun/uno), [Tic-Tac-Toe](/docs/custom-bot/modules/fun/tic-tak-toe),
  [Rock Paper Scissors](/docs/custom-bot/modules/fun/rock-paper-scissors),
  [Emoji Quiz](/docs/custom-bot/modules/fun/emoji-quiz), [Flag Quiz](/docs/custom-bot/modules/fun/flag-quiz),
  [giveaways](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/giveaways), [polls](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/polls), and
  more.
* **Community Engagement** — [Suggestions](/docs/custom-bot/modules/administration/suggestions),
  [starboard](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/starboard),
  [activity streaks](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/activity-streak),
  [invite tracking](/docs/custom-bot/modules/moderation/invite-tracking),
  [team goals](/docs/custom-bot/modules/administration/team-goals), and
  [reminders](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/reminders).
* **Server Management** — [Live stat channels](/docs/custom-bot/modules/administration/channel-stats),
  [scheduled messages](/docs/custom-bot/modules/tools/auto-messager),
  [auto-publisher](/docs/custom-bot/modules/tools/auto-publisher),
  [auto-delete](/docs/custom-bot/modules/administration/auto-delete),
  [sticky messages](/docs/custom-bot/modules/community/sticky-messages),

  [admin tools](/docs/custom-bot/modules/administration/admin-tools).
* **Custom Commands** — Create your own slash commands, button interactions, modals, and message triggers — all without

* **Bot Log-In** — Send messages and embeds as your bot, create self-role messages — all from the dashboard. See the
  [Bot Log-In documentation](/docs/custom-bot/login-as-bot) for details.

## Getting started {#getting-started}

1. **Set up your bot** — Follow the [setup guide](/docs/custom-bot) to create your Discord application, generate a
   token, and connect your bot to SCNX.
2. **Enable modules** — Open the [module manager](https://scnx.app/glink?page=bot/modules) and enable the features you
   want.
3. **Configure your bot** — Customize module settings, messages, and behavior in
   the [configuration panel](https://scnx.app/glink?page=bot/configuration).
4. **Set up permissions** — Configure who can use which commands. See
   the [Slash Commands & Permissions guide](/docs/custom-bot/slash-commands).
